## Why

Editorial feedback: "I need the story to capture me first and make me live the protagonist's shoes... Strong narrative > roleplaying mechanic."

Multi-perspective creative panel evaluation concluded:
- **Structure:** 4-5 cosmetic beats (compressed) earn character investment before meaningful choice
- **Interiority:** Automatic voice (not player-gated) preserves narrative spell
- **Consequence:** Awareness shapes action — "seeing clearly" = agency (core pedagogical thesis)
