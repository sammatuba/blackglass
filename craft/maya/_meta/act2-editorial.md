## Overall Act 2 Learnings

### Writer Persona Strengths Discovered

**Minimalist:**
- Best at: Mathematical precision, clean consequence paths, white space as weight
- Weakness: Emotional distance, clinical tone sacrifices player investment
- Use for: Choice structure clarity, concept visibility through numbers, ending beats

**Storyteller:**
- Best at: Cultural texture, relationship grounding, sensory detail, warmth
- Weakness: Can be too comfortable, pacing occasionally drags
- Use for: Family GC dynamics, character voice authenticity, emotional anchoring

**Thriller:**
- Best at: Urgency, real-time stakes, pacing, dread escalation
- Weakness: Paranoia can overwhelm learning, Maya can feel passive/hunted
- Use for: Opening hooks, crisis moments, time pressure sequences

**Empathist:**
- Best at: Interiority, moral complexity, emotional weight, "both choices hurt"
- Weakness: Can linger too long in feelings, risks navel-gazing
- Use for: Choice moments, consequence processing, character transformation beats

**Satirist:**
- Best at: Systemic framing, dark humor, meta-awareness, concept visibility without lecture
- Weakness: Humor can distance emotion if overused, observational tone risks breaking POV
- Use for: Algorithm-as-character, tonal relief, making the invisible visible

**Scene 3 (Feed Knows You):**

**Scene 4 (Family GC):**
