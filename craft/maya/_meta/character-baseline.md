## 1. MAYA'S CHARACTER STATE AT END OF ACT 1

### What Maya Knows (Facts)

**About Digital Deception:**
- GCash "wrong send" scams exist and use fake push notifications (if she checked balance or googled)
- Health misinformation spreads through family networks via trusted relatives
- Deepfake/synthetic media exists on social media (celebrity crypto endorsements)
- Scammers use personalization (her name in SMS) to build false legitimacy
- Multiple scam types can arrive in a single morning without obvious pattern

**About Her Own Vulnerability:**
- She clicks links reflexively when they come from family ("automation bias" seeded)
- Her financial situation is precarious (₱2,341.17 GCash balance, freelance income uncertainty)
- She's susceptible to urgency + emotional manipulation (sick child narrative)
- Her phone is the first thing she reaches for — "muscle memory since she was fifteen"

**About Family Dynamics:**
- Tita Merly shares health misinformation with sincere belief ("my officemate sent this, doctor daw ang source")
- Kuya Renz/Cousin Jep are the family skeptics who question links
- Mama defers to Maya's judgment but stays neutral in GC disputes
- Family GC mixes mundane (pasabuy requests) with urgent (health warnings) with sacred (blessing chains)

### What Maya Believes (Attitudes)

**About Trust:**
- Family love and digital caution exist in painful tension
- Questioning elders' shared content feels like disrespect (hiya/shame dynamic)
- To verify = to distrust = to become the "cold" person she doesn't want to be
- "Feeling is faster than checking" (from Empathist draft — core truth about her decision-making)

**About Family:**
- "The family comes first. It always does." (Opening scene choice text)
- Tita Merly "means well" even when sharing misinformation
- Silence is easier than correction in family GC ("heart react and move on")
- Family requests create obligations that feel like love ("type AMEN!" blessing chains)

**About Technology:**
- Her phone is both lifeline (work, family, income) and threat (scams, manipulation)
- Apps are trustworthy authorities (GCash logo = legitimacy)
- The feed is something you scroll "the way you open a fridge — not because you're hungry but because your hand already knows the way"
- Notifications create "small obligations" in her chest

**About Work/Money:**
- Freelance deadlines are non-negotiable ("client wants revisions by monday" on Saturday)
- Money is always tight (₱5,000 = groceries for two weeks / rent significance)
- "Tight budget big dreams" is the freelance reality
- Work pressure happens at all hours (6:31 AM project brief)

### Established Relationships

**Tita Merly (Aunt):**
- Relationship: Warm, loving, elder who must be respected
- Communication pattern: Early morning messages (5:31 AM - 6:02 AM), shares health/wellness content, uses blessing chains
- Maya's response: Heart reacts, gentle questioning with emojis to soften, rarely direct confrontation
- Key dynamic: Tita vouches for content sincerity ("officemate ko, reliable naman yun. Nurse sya before")

**Kuya Renz / Cousin Jep (Family Skeptic):**
- Relationship: Protective older cousin/brother figure
- Communication pattern: Drops in GC with practical requests (lumpia recipe), warns Maya about scams

- Key quote: "CHECK. THE. BALANCE." (all caps urgency against Tita's emotion)

**Mama:**
- Relationship: Central authority, warm but defers to Maya's judgment
- Communication pattern: "Kumain ka na anak", "gising ka na?", neutral in GC disputes
- Maya's response: Immediate reply ("Good morning po! 😊 Gising na"), wants approval
- Key dynamic: Mama thanked Tita Merly for health link before Maya could question it (social proof pressure)

**Papa:**
- Presence: Mentioned but not directly communicating in Act 1
- Context: Has high blood pressure (Tita's link "lalo na ikaw Papa Santos!")
- Implication: Health vulnerability in family increases pressure to believe wellness content

**Bea 💛 (Best Friend):**
- Relationship: Close friend, peer support
- Communication pattern: All-caps excitement, sends TikTok/viral videos, asks for advice
- Maya's response: Warm, casual, shares laugh emojis

**Work GC (PixelPush Projects: Jigs, Anya, Mikko):**

- Anya = minimal communication ("F")

### Choices Made & What They Reveal

**Possible Choice Paths (Player-Dependent):**

*If Maya clicked Tita Merly's health link:*
- She prioritizes family connection over digital skepticism
- Curiosity/obligation wins over caution
- She's learning health misinformation feels "normal" when wrapped in care

*If Maya asked Tita where the link came from:*

*If Maya googled the scam:*
- She exercised "human-in-command" — pausing automated responses
- She learned scams are documented, patterns are knowable
- She may feel guilty for needing proof ("what if it was real?")

## 4. AI CONCEPTS ALREADY INTRODUCED

### Automation Bias (Seeded, Not Named)

**Definition (Implicit):** The tendency to trust automated/digital information without verification because it looks official or comes from a familiar interface.

**Where Seeded:**

*Scene 1:*
- Tita Merly's link has "medical-looking header, stock photos of worried doctors" (visual authority)
- GLife SMS has "God bless!" making it "feel more human. Like someone actually typed it."
- Celebrity crypto video: "The smile was perfect. The eye contact was unwavering. The mouth synced to every syllable. It was the most convincing thing Maya had seen all morning."
- Variable tracking: `automation_bias_seeds++` for clicking without verification

*Scene 2:*
- GCash notification looks real (green, logo, familiar UI)
- Fake GCash site "looked almost right — the GLife logo, the green, the layout"
- Push notification = assumed legitimacy (system-generated = truthful)

**How Shown:**
- Maya (and player) trust interfaces that mimic authority
- Logos, colors, formatting create false sense of security
- The "almost right" sites slip past casual verification
- "Feeling is faster than checking" (Empathist draft key line)

**Concept Card Language (Scene 2 End):**
- "APPROPRIATE TRUST — Kindness and caution aren't opposites. Verifying doesn't make you cruel."
- "Real emergencies survive a five-second balance check."

### Contextual Blindness (Seeded, Not Named)

**Definition (Implicit):** Failure to evaluate content critically when it comes from a trusted source or familiar context.

**Where Seeded:**

*Scene 1:*
- Family GC mixes blessing chains, pasabuy requests, and misinformation seamlessly
- Tita Merly's health link "sits there between the blessed-morning image and the pasabuy request, looking like everything else"
- Mama's thank-you creates social proof before Maya can question
- Variable tracking: `contextual_blindness_seeds++` for family-first choices

**How Shown:**
- Trusted messenger (Tita Merly) makes untrusted content (wellness-truth.ph) feel safe
- Vouching chains ("my officemate", "nurse sya before", "friend from church") substitute for evidence
- Questioning content = questioning relationship (social cost too high)
- "The link was now two hearts deep in a chain of trust" (Storyteller draft)

**Cultural Amplification:**
- Utang na loob (debt of gratitude to elders who care)
- Hiya (shame of implying elder is wrong/foolish)
- Pakikisama (group harmony over individual correctness)

### Human-in-Command (Introduced as Practice, Not Term)

**Definition (Implicit):** Maintaining agency to pause, verify, and question automated/digital processes before acting.

**Where Introduced:**

*Scene 2:*
- Choice to check GCash balance before sending money

## 5. CULTURAL AUTHENTICITY MARKERS

### Utang na Loob (Debt of Gratitude)

**Definition:** Reciprocal obligation stemming from received kindness; debts are moral, not transactional.

**Where Manifest:**

*Scene 1:*
- Tita Merly sends blessed morning GIF daily (establishes pattern of care)
- Sharing health link is framed as gift ("para aware lahat 💕")
- Not thanking/questioning = rejecting the gift = rejecting the love
- Mama's immediate "Salamat Ate Merly! ❤️" models expected gratitude response

*Scene 2:*
- Scammer leverages this: "I know this is a lot to ask from a stranger" (acknowledges debt-creation)
- "Keep ₱2,000 for your trouble" (offering to create mutual obligation)
- Maya's internal thought: "Mama, who raised three kids on a market vendor's salary. You think of every time someone helped your family when no one had to."

**Decision Impact:**
- Heart-reacting Tita's link = honoring debt of care
- Sending money to scammer = honoring imagined debt to suffering mother
- Refusing to help = becoming "the kind of person you swore you'd never be"

**Narrative Weight:**
- Makes verification feel like betrayal
- Creates complicity through gratitude
- "To question the link was to question the friend. To question the friend was to question the church. To question the church was to question Tita Merly." (Empathist draft)

### Hiya (Shame/Propriety)

**Definition:** Sense of social propriety; avoiding actions that cause loss of face (yours or others').

**Where Manifest:**

*Scene 1:*
- Adding emoji to questioning message ("Tita, let me check where this is from? 😊") to soften potential offense
- Heart-reacting instead of questioning ("the path of least resistance... also the path of most love")
- Tita Peachy's gentle mockery ("Naku Merly ano na naman yan 😂") acceptable; direct confrontation is not

*Scene 2:*
- "Checking feels like an accusation. Like saying: I don't believe you. To a mother. About her sick daughter."
- Shame at feeling relief when scam is confirmed ("shame at the relief")
- If scammed: won't tell Mama, eats less and claims dieting (hiding shame)

**Decision Impact:**
- The emoji becomes load-bearing (without it, message sounds like doubt)
- Silence = preserving everyone's face
- Being scammed is less shameful than being perceived as cruel/suspicious

**Satirist Draft Meta-Commentary:**
- Tita dismisses Maya's skepticism: "Ikaw talaga, palagi kang nag-o-overthink 😂"
- "She had been diagnosed: overthinker. Case closed." (skepticism reframed as personal flaw)

### Pakikisama (Smooth Interpersonal Relations)

**Definition:** Going along to get along; prioritizing group harmony over individual stance.

**Where Manifest:**

*Scene 1:*
- Heart-reacting to avoid disrupting family GC flow
- "Type AMEN!" blessing chains (participation = belonging)
- Tita Peachy's pasabuy request runs parallel to health misinformation (both require yes)

*Scene 2 Family GC Dynamics:*
- Tita Merly frames compassion as group value ("do unto others")
- Kuya Jep's warnings risk being seen as "laging negative" (disrupting harmony)
- Mama's "Mag-ingat ka Maya ha" preserves neutrality (doesn't take sides)

**Tension Points:**
- Kuya Jep chooses individual correctness over group harmony (pays social cost)
- Maya must choose: pakikisama (agree with Tita) or verification (side with Jep)
- "With all due respect" = formula for disagreeing while maintaining surface harmony

**Narrative Weight:**
- Misinformation spreads because correction breaks harmony
- Scams succeed because refusal feels antisocial
- "The Santos Family GC is warm and ordinary" = this is how normal feels

**Observed Patterns:**

*Content Mix (Seamless Transitions):*
- Sacred: Blessed morning GIFs, "Type AMEN!", Bible quotes
- Mundane: Pasabuy requests, recipe requests, "kumain ka na?"

*Participation Expectations:*
- Elders post frequently (Tita Merly 5:31 AM start time)
- Younger members respond (acknowledgment expected)
- Silence is noted ("Wala pa kayong reply ha 😤")

*Authority Dynamics:*
- Elders share first, younger members respond

- "My officemate" → "Nurse sya before" → "Marami na daw natulungan"

## 6. VOICE & STYLE CONTINUITY NOTES

### Maya's Internal Voice (Composite of Draft Personas)

**From Empathist Draft (Primary):**
- Feelings precede thoughts ("Her chest does the thing it does")
- Self-aware about emotional conflicts ("Relief first. Then: shame at the relief.")
- Questions her own goodness ("When did she become the person who reads a mother's plea and thinks: 'Is this real?'")
- Metaphorical thinking ("The ceiling was white and quiet and didn't want anything from her")

**From Storyteller Draft:**
- Warm observations ("The chat is warm and ordinary")
- Specific sensory details ("One sharp vibration against wood")
- Community-aware ("Aling Nena from your barangay fell for this last month")

**From Satirist Draft (Use Sparingly):**
- Occasional wry meta-awareness ("The emoji was doing a lot of heavy lifting")
- Dark humor about systems ("The algorithm's timing was impeccable")
- Pattern recognition ("This is the Fibonacci sequence of Filipino guilt")

**Avoid:**
- Minimalist detachment (too cold for sustained POV)
- Thriller pacing (tension over interiority)

### Tense & POV Consistency

**Established in Polished Scripts:**
- **Tense:** Present tense ("The screen glows before your eyes are fully open")
- **POV:** Second person ("You reach for it the way you always do")
- **Effect:** Immediacy + implication (player IS Maya, not watching her)

**Internal Thought Handling:**
- Unmarked interiority woven into action ("Your thumb hovers. Saturday morning. Nowhere to be yet.")
- Occasional direct address to self ("Wait. What number?")
- Avoid italics/thought tags unless necessary for clarity

### Pacing & Beat Structure

**From Polished Scripts:**
- Notification arrives → Maya observes → Choice moment → Consequence
- Beat pauses (`# BEAT: 2s`) create breath between rapid-fire messages
- Sound cues (`# SOUND: gcash_chime`) anchor emotional timing
- Typing indicators build tension before scammer replies

**Scene Structure:**
- Open with sensory anchor (screen glow, phone buzz, notification light)
- Choice hubs offer 3-4 options (no "correct" path telegraphed)
- Exit points marked with `# EXIT_POINT` when player escapes scam/harm
- Concept cards appear AFTER consequence, never before choice
