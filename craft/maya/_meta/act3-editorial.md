### Creative Decisions

**Opening Beat:** Chose post-dinner timing (culturally authentic) with dishes cleared but family lingering. Electric fan as recurring sensory anchor grounds scene physically.

**Maya's Rehearsal:** Kept Empathist's "been rehearsing all afternoon" because it shows the emotional labor of this conversation—not spontaneous but planned, which makes vulnerability more affecting.

**Evidence Sequence:** Deepfake → email scam → SIM advisory. This order moves from abstract (video they all saw) to personal (Maya's near-loss) to Kuya's complicity (hardest emotionally). Escalating stakes.

**Kuya Renz Arc:** His defensiveness → realization → quiet acceptance is the scene's emotional hinge. "I thought I was being careful" captures the core horror: careful people get targeted *because* they're careful.

**Papa's Validation:** His rare speech and physical action (placing phone on table face-up) validates Maya without solving anything. He doesn't understand the tech—he understands his daughter needs support.

**Mama's "Teach Me":** Most earned in Empathist draft. Her questions ("what is a URL?") show the gap isn't willingness but knowledge. The teaching becomes two-way—Maya learns she can't explain everything, family learns they can't trust everything.

**Cultural Authenticity Markers:**
- Tinola (specific dish, not generic "dinner")
- "Diyos ko" as Mama's shock response
- Kuya's loss-of-face moment (forwarded to three GCs)
- Papa's silence until it matters (generational/gender dynamic)
- Rooster at 8PM (Filipino neighborhood texture)
- Fan as constant (not AC—economic reality)

**Teaching Invisibility:** Concepts never named. "Deepfake" used only because family would use that word (it's entered common parlance). "AI-generated" is Maya's explanation. "Proxy targeting" remains invisible—just shown as "they study what we click."

## Scene 10: The Reckoning Call

### Draft Rankings & Scores

1. **Draft 1 (Empathist)** — 74/80
   - Voice: 14/15, Tension: 12/15, Cultural: 13/15, Emotional: 15/15, Choice: 10/10, Teaching: 10/10
2. **Draft 2 (Storyteller)** — 72/80
   - Voice: 14/15, Tension: 11/15, Cultural: 14/15, Emotional: 14/15, Choice: 9/10, Teaching: 10/10
3. **Draft 3 (Minimalist)** — 68/80
   - Voice: 11/15, Tension: 12/15, Cultural: 12/15, Emotional: 11/15, Choice: 10/10, Teaching: 12/10 (extra point for clarity)

### Composite Strategy

**Backbone:** Draft 1 (Empathist) — interiority and emotional weight strongest

**Imported Elements:**
- Draft 2: Sensory grounding (Saturday morning light, memory annotations)
- Draft 3: Mathematical precision (6 numbers, 23 emails, 4 reports)
- Draft 1: Choice cost analysis (every option hurts)

**Final Score:** 76/80

### Creative Decisions

**Opening:** Quiet Saturday morning after crisis. No notifications = intentional contrast to Act 2's bombardment.

**Inventory Structure:** Blocked numbers (6), deleted emails (23), reported posts (4), GCash balance (₱4,247). Concrete evidence of the week's toll.

**The List:** Eight rules Maya drafts. Each is both true and insufficient. The list represents her attempt to systematize the unsystematic.

**Three-Choice Structure:** Share list (public/family/private), social media (delete/deactivate/keep), report (NBI/platforms/nothing). Nine possible paths, all showing agency without triumph.

**No Path Resolution:** Chose to composite around "keep but change habits" (screen time limit, notification control) because it's the most realistic/unsatisfying choice. Deletion would be too dramatic. Keeping unchanged would contradict growth.

**Final Addition:** "This is the work now. Forever." Maya adds 9th rule herself. The permanence is the learning.

**Teaching Invisibility:** "Human-in-command" never named but demonstrated through each choice Maya makes (pause, verify, set boundaries).

### What Worked

- Empathist's cost enumeration strongest
- Minimalist's math (blocked: 6, deleted: 23) made toll concrete
- Storyteller's memory-anchored list (each rule = specific scene callback)
- All drafts avoided triumphant tone

### What Didn't

- Minimalist's emotional distance when Maya needs to feel the weight
- Some choice paths felt too theoretical in Empathist draft
- Could have used more sensory grounding throughout

### Guardrails Compliance

✓ **Complicity before clarity:** Maya acts (limits, curates) before fully understanding if it helps  
✓ **Phone is real:** Screen time settings, notification controls, app deletion all standard  
✓ **No villains:** System is adversary, not people  
✓ **Concept invisible:** Appropriate trust, agency demonstrated not named  
✓ **Both choices hurt:** Share = vulnerability. Keep = responsibility. All cost.

### Preservation Lines

- "This is the work now. Forever." (Empathist — thesis)
- "Knowing doesn't fix anything. It just makes her responsible." (Empathist)
- "If I feel stupid for questioning, that's the manipulation working." (All drafts)
- "For when I forget." (Empathist — note title)

## What We Learned: Persona Performance in Act 3

**Empathist:**
- Won or tied for top rank in Scenes 9, 10, 11, and 3/4 endings
- Strength: Emotional interiority, vulnerability, cost enumeration
- Weakness: Can linger in feelings (needed trimming in places)
- Best use: Character transformation beats, moral complexity, endings

**Storyteller:**
- Highest cultural authenticity scores across all scenes
- Strength: Sensory detail, relationship texture, warmth
- Weakness: Pacing drags when comfort overwhelms tension
- Best use: Cultural grounding, family dynamics, sensory anchoring

**Minimalist:**
- Strongest in mathematical precision, structural clarity
- Strength: White space as weight, clean consequence paths
- Weakness: Emotional distance reduces player investment
- Best use: Closing beats, choice structure, concept visibility through metrics

**Thriller:**
- Highest tension scores but rarely top-ranked overall
- Strength: Urgency, crisis pacing, dread escalation
- Weakness: Paranoia can overwhelm learning, Maya feels hunted vs. trapped
- Best use: Opening hooks, time pressure, specific urgency beats (not sustained POV)

**Satirist:**
- Consistent mid-high scores, never bottom-ranked
- Strength: Systemic framing, dark humor, tonal balance, meta-awareness
- Weakness: Humor risks distancing emotion if overused
- Best use: Making invisible visible, tonal relief, observational clarity

**Optimal Composite Pattern (Validated):**
Primary persona (60-70%) + Secondary texture (20-30%) + Tertiary punctuation (10-20%)
